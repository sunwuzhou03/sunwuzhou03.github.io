  • CPU efficiency=${Q\over Q+S}$
  • CPU efficiency=${S\over S+S}$=$50\%$
  • CPU efficiency=${T\over T+N*S}$=0($N\rightarrow\infty$)

2.

  • When we give 1 recorder to 1 process then total process will be 6 but in this case there will definitely deadlock occur because every process contain 1 recorder and waiting for another recorder which is hold by other process therefore when we reduce 1 process then system to be deadlock free.
    Hence, the maximum value of n is 5 and the minimum value is n is 1.

3.

  • Mutual exclusion: Only one progress may use a resource at a time.
  • Hold and wait: A progress may hold allocated resources while awaiting assignment of others.
  • No preemption: No resource can be forcibly removed from a process holding it.
  • circular wait: A closed chain of progresses exits, such that each process holds at least one resource needed by the next process in the chain.
