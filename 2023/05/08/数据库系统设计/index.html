设计一个健身房器材销售数据库。

关系模式

标黑，下划线为主键

  • 老板（老板id，姓名，性别，电话）

  • 员工（员工id，姓名，性别，电话，老板id），老板id为外键

  • 商店（商店id，名字，地址，营业时间）

  • 管理（老板id，商店id），商店id为外键

  • 雇佣（商店id，员工id），员工id为外键

  • 客户（客户id，姓名，性别，电话）

  • 器材（器材id，功能，价格，保修期）

  • 厂商（厂商id，名字，地址）

  • 购买（订单id，客户id，器材id，商店id，器材数量，下单时间），客户id，器材id，商店id为外键

  • 进货（订单id，厂商id，器材id，商店id，器材数量，下单时间），客户id，器材id，商店id为外键

  • 存储（商店id，器材id，数量），器材id，商店id为外键

关系模式范式分解

转换为英文

  • Boss (boss_id, boss_name, gender, phone number)
  • Employee (employee_id, employee_name, gender, phone number, boss_id), boss_id is a foreign key
  • Store (store_id, store_name, store_address, business hours)
  • Management (boss_id, store_id), store_id is a foreign key
  • Employment (store_id, employee_id), employee_id is a foreign key
  • Customer (customer_id, customer_name, gender, phone number)
  • Equipment (equipment_id, equipment_name, function, price, warranty_period)
  • Manufacturer (manufacturer_id, name, manufacturer_address)
  • Purchase (order_id, customer_id, equipment_id, store_id, equipment_quantity, order time), customer_id, equipment_id, store_id are foreign keys
  • Stock (order_id, manufacturer_id, equipment_id, store_id, equipment_quantity, order time), customer_id, equipment_id, store_id are foreign keys
  • Storage (store_id, equipment_id, quantity), equipment_id, store_id are foreign keys.
